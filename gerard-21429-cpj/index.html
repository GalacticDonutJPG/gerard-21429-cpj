The popularity of virtual reality (VR) has grown exponentially in recent years, with advancements in technology and software enabling more sophisticated and immersive experiences. In a society where we are so connected that we have become disconnected, can musicians and music creators find belonging and ease social anxiety with the help of VR?

This website will provide an overview of the potential use of virtual reality to create experiences through music and foster meaningful communities.
